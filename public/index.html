Welcome to Inventory Management API

Explore the available endpoints and user roles below.

View Swagger API Docs
Admin
  • Manage users and roles
  • Manage all products, categories, suppliers, warehouses
  • View/manage all orders, transactions, reports
  • Set pricing, discounts, promotions
  • Full system access
Manager
  • View/approve/reject orders
  • View sales & inventory reports
  • Oversee inventory & warehouse ops
  • Coordinate sales, warehouse, suppliers
Warehouse
  • View/update inventory levels
  • Manage product storage & stock
  • Process shipments & orders
  • Track inventory across warehouses
Sales
  • View products & inventory
  • Create/manage customer orders
  • Track order status
  • Shipping orders
Supplier
  • View/update own product listings
  • Manage stock supplied to warehouse
  • View orders for their products
  • Update product availability & pricing
